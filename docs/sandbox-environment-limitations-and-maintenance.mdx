Below is a summary of the key limitations associated with our partner, WorldPay:

**WorldPay Sandbox Limitations**

Data Retention Policy:

* Data is retained for a maximum of **30 days**.
* Depending on overall system capacity and maintenance requirements, data purges may occur more frequently. Advanced notification will be provided whenever possible.

Maintenance Windows:

* General maintenance: Tuesdays and Thursdays from 4:00 AM to 8:00 AM ET.
* Enterprise eProtect maintenance: Every other Thursday from 10:00 PM to 6:00 AM ET.

Transaction Limits:

* Daily limit of 1,000 online transactions (shared capacity, so actual limits may be lower).
* Daily limit of 10,000 batch transactions.

For the latest information on WorldPay sandbox limitations, refer to their official documentation:

[WorldPay Sandbox Limits](http://support.worldpay.com/support/CNP-API/content/preliveenvlimits.htm)
